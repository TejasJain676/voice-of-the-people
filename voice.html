Raise Your Voice

Steps for citizens to document issues, file complaints, and use RTI.

File Complaint

Official portals to register issues and seek redressal:

CPGRAMS (Centralized Public Grievance Redress and Monitoring System): Lodge grievances with any Central or State Government department.
Link: https://pgportal.gov.in/
National Consumer Helpline (NCH): Register complaints regarding unfair trade practices, goods, and services (under the Department of Consumer Affairs).
Link: https://consumerhelpline.gov.in/
SCORES (SEBI Complaint Redress System): Lodge investor complaints against entities regulated by the Securities and Exchange Board of India.
Link: https://scores.sebi.gov.in/
Bima Bharosa Portal (IRDAI): Register grievances related to insurance claims and policies (managed by the Insurance Regulatory and Development Authority of India).
Link: https://bimabharosa.irdai.gov.in/
